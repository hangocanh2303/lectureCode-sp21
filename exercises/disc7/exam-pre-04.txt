4 ADT Selection
Suppose we have a TA Shreyas who teaches multiple discussion sections! A student
may frequent more than one discussion section. For each situation below, choose
the best ADT(s) out of the following — Map, Set, List — and explain how you can
use the ADT(s) to solve the problem. Each subpart is independent of the previous.
One answer may involve multiple ADTs. There may be mutliple efficient answers
for each problem.
1. Storing all the Students in Shreyas’s first section in alphabetical order.
List
2. Storing all the Students by their section, where Students within a section are
sorted alphabetically.
Map<section, List<Student>)
3. Storing the Students in all of Shreyas’s sections. There shouldn’t be duplicates.
Set
4. Determining all the Students that attend more than one of Shreyas’s sections.
Map<student, count>
5. Quickly getting a Student by sid.
Map<sid, student>
6. Quickly getting a Student by name or sid. Names aren’t necessarily unique.
twos map
7. Cycling through the Students in one discussion section.
get list student by section and loop
